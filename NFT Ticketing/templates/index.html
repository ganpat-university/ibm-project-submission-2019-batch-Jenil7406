What is NFT Ticketing

It is an innovative to implement to ticketing concept to provide transparency and trust.

NFT tickets are based on ERC-721 contract of Ethereum. They signify proof of payment and the right to entry to events and experiences. The process of issuing NFT tickets is also fairly simple.

Our Features

Prevent forgery

NFT tickets cannot be faked or forged. This is because they are minted on the blockchain which provides a tamperproof record of ownership

Transparent System

Since the Tickets are on blockchain, event organizers and attendees can validate the authenticity of every ticket on the chain and track the history of ownership
